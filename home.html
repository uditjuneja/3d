We specialize in:

  • (a) creating premium-quality, Photo realistic 3D Product Renders and Animations used for Investor’s Pitch, Marketing, Branding and Pre-Visualizations.
  • (b) Cleaning up 3D Scans ready to be used for AR, VR, and 3D Printing Companies.

With a team of 15 3D Artists and collective experience of over 50 Years in various Computer Generated Imagery (CGI) fields, we believe in going above and beyond for our clients. Hence, the name Ikarus 3D.

3D Scan Cleanups​

Get your 3D Scans cleaned up. This service is a must for Mixed Reality and 3D Printing companies as well as people who want to use 3D scanning to showcase their products on the websites. From People to Products to Places, we clean them all.

3D Product Renders

Revolutionize your Customer Experience with our High-quality images of your product using CGI to help you with Marketing and Branding.

AR, VR and Gaming Assets

Get your custom asset library. With our large team of 3D Artists no number of assets is too high.

3D Printable Models

Get High-end custom 3D Print ready 3D Models. Practical Prints for Mechanical and Collectible items. We take care of the details to emphasize the unique qualities of your projects.

Character Modelling

Get custom characters made pulled directly from your imagination to the digital realm. We specialize in Character Modelling for Gaming as well as Animated movies.

Fashion Visualization​

Much like Product Visualizations revolutionize your Customer Experience with our High-quality true-to-life 3D garment simulation using CGI to help you with Marketing and Branding.
